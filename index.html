Binary Doodles

Our team, Binary Doodles, is one of the student teams in Distributed Software Develpment 2020 of Jilin University and we have some excellent students who have participated in ACM competition. A Google Doodles is a set of interesting logos on Google's homepages, and we are inspired by this to name our team Binary Doodles beacuse we wish our developing software process is as interesting as drawing doodles. Welcome Portuguese students to join us!
